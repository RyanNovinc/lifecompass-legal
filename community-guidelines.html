Community Guidelines - LifeCompass

Last Updated: January 2025

These Community Guidelines govern your participation in LifeCompass community features, including our Discord server and any other community platforms we may provide access to.

1. OVERVIEW AND ACCEPTANCE

1.1 Purpose

These Guidelines promote a positive, supportive environment where LifeCompass users can connect, share experiences, and help each other achieve their goals.

1.2 Scope

These Guidelines apply to:

  • LifeCompass Discord server
  • Any official LifeCompass community platforms
  • Interactions between community members that relate to LifeCompass

1.3 Agreement

By participating in LifeCompass communities, you agree to follow these Guidelines in addition to:

  • LifeCompass Terms of Service
  • LifeCompass Privacy Policy
  • Third-party platform terms (Discord Terms of Service, etc.)

2. COMMUNITY PRINCIPLES

2.1 Supportive Environment

  • Encourage and support fellow community members
  • Celebrate achievements and progress, no matter how small
  • Offer constructive feedback when requested
  • Respect different approaches to goal achievement

2.2 Respect and Inclusion

  • Treat all community members with dignity and respect
  • Welcome newcomers and help them feel included
  • Respect diverse backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives
  • Use inclusive language and avoid discriminatory content

2.3 Constructive Communication

  • Keep discussions relevant to productivity, goals, and personal development
  • Share experiences and insights that may help others
  • Ask thoughtful questions and provide helpful responses
  • Maintain a positive and solution-focused approach

3. SHARING ADVICE AND INFORMATION

3.1 Important Disclaimer

Community discussions are for educational and experience-sharing purposes only and do not constitute professional advice. Users should consult qualified professionals for personalized guidance regarding:

  • Financial matters: Financial advisors, accountants, or financial planners
  • Health and wellness: Healthcare professionals, doctors, or licensed practitioners
  • Mental health: Licensed therapists, counselors, or mental health professionals
  • Business and legal: Business lawyers, accountants, or professional consultants

3.2 Sharing Personal Experiences

  • Share what has worked for you personally
  • Clearly indicate when sharing personal opinions vs. facts
  • Acknowledge that results may vary for different people
  • Encourage others to do their own research

4. PROHIBITED CONTENT AND BEHAVIOR

4.1 Prohibited Content

  • Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
  • Hate speech, discrimination, or offensive content
  • Spam, promotional content, or self-promotion
  • Illegal activities or content
  • Sharing personal financial information or credentials
  • Medical advice or specific health recommendations
  • Investment advice or specific financial recommendations

4.2 Prohibited Behavior

  • Impersonating others or providing false information
  • Sharing inappropriate or explicit content
  • Attempting to recruit members for external services
  • Violating third-party platform rules (Discord, etc.)

5. ENFORCEMENT

5.1 Violations

Violations of these guidelines may result in:

  • Warnings or temporary restrictions
  • Removal from community platforms
  • Suspension or termination of your LifeCompass account
  • Reporting to third-party platforms or authorities where appropriate

5.2 Reporting

To report violations or concerns, please:

  • Use the reporting features on the respective platform (Discord, etc.)
  • Contact LifeCompass support through the app
  • Provide specific details about the violation

6. CONTACT INFORMATION

For questions about these Community Guidelines, please contact us through the LifeCompass app support feature.
