LIFECOMPASS COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Last Updated: September 2025

These Community Guidelines govern your participation in LifeCompass community features, including our Discord server and any other community platforms we may provide access to.

---

1. OVERVIEW AND ACCEPTANCE

1.1 Purpose

These Guidelines promote a positive, supportive environment where LifeCompass users can connect, share experiences, and help each other achieve their goals.

1.2 Scope

These Guidelines apply to:

  • LifeCompass Discord server
  • Any official LifeCompass community platforms
  • Interactions between community members that relate to LifeCompass

1.3 Agreement

By participating in LifeCompass communities, you agree to follow these Guidelines in addition to:

  • LifeCompass Terms of Service
  • LifeCompass Privacy Policy
  • Third-party platform terms (Discord Terms of Service, etc.)

---

2. COMMUNITY PRINCIPLES

2.1 Supportive Environment

  • Encourage and support fellow community members
  • Celebrate achievements and progress, no matter how small
  • Offer constructive feedback when requested
  • Respect different approaches to goal achievement

2.2 Respect and Inclusion

  • Treat all community members with dignity and respect
  • Welcome newcomers and help them feel included
  • Respect diverse backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives
  • Use inclusive language and avoid discriminatory content

2.3 Constructive Communication

  • Keep discussions relevant to productivity, goals, and personal development
  • Share experiences and insights that may help others
  • Ask thoughtful questions and provide helpful responses
  • Maintain a positive and solution-focused approach

---

3. SHARING ADVICE AND INFORMATION

3.1 Personal Experience Sharing

✅ Encouraged:

  • Sharing your personal experiences with goal achievement
  • Describing strategies that have worked for you
  • Offering general productivity tips and resources
  • Sharing motivational content and success stories

3.2 Professional Advice Limitations

❌ Not Permitted:

  • Providing financial advice or specific investment recommendations
  • Offering medical, health, or mental health advice for specific conditions
  • Giving legal advice or interpretation
  • Career coaching without appropriate qualifications
  • Tax or accounting guidance
  • Specific parenting advice for behavioral or developmental issues
  • Business legal advice or regulatory guidance

3.3 Advice Disclaimers

When sharing experiences or suggestions:

  • Make clear you're sharing personal experience, not professional advice
  • Encourage others to do their own research
  • Recommend consulting qualified professionals for important decisions
  • Acknowledge that what worked for you may not work for everyone

Examples:

  • *"This budgeting method worked well for me, but everyone's situation is different. You might want to talk to a financial advisor about what's best for your specific circumstances."*
  • *"This fitness routine helped me, but I'm just sharing my experience - not fitness advice. Always consult a trainer or doctor before starting new exercise programs."*
  • *"This parenting approach worked for our family, but every child is different. For specific behavioral concerns, a pediatrician or child psychologist would be much more helpful than my experience."*

---

4. INFORMATION SHARING AND PRIVACY

4.1 Personal Information

❌ Never Share:

  • Full names, addresses, or phone numbers
  • Financial account numbers or passwords
  • Social Security numbers or other government IDs
  • Specific salary amounts or detailed financial information
  • Medical records, detailed health information, or mental health details

⚠️ Share With Caution:

  • General location (city/state okay, specific address not okay)
  • Job titles or industry (avoid company names)
  • General financial goals (avoid specific dollar amounts)
  • Health and fitness goals (avoid medical details or mental health specifics)
  • General parenting experiences (avoid specific child behavioral or developmental details)

4.2 Privacy Reminders

  • Remember that community discussions are visible to all members
  • Third-party platforms (like Discord) have their own privacy policies
  • Screenshots of conversations may be shared outside the community
  • Consider whether you'd be comfortable with your information being public

4.3 Reporting Privacy Concerns

If someone shares inappropriate personal information:

  • Report it using platform tools (Discord report feature)
  • Contact community moderators
  • Don't screenshot or share the information further

---

5. PROHIBITED CONTENT AND BEHAVIOR

5.1 Harassment and Bullying

❌ Prohibited:

  • Personal attacks, insults, or harassment
  • Discriminatory language or hate speech
  • Doxxing (sharing someone's personal information)
  • Threats or intimidation
  • Persistent unwanted contact

5.2 Inappropriate Content

❌ Prohibited:

  • Spam, excessive self-promotion, or advertising
  • Adult content or sexually explicit material
  • Violence, illegal activity, or dangerous behavior
  • Misinformation or deliberately false information
  • Content that violates platform policies

5.3 Commercial Activities

❌ Prohibited:

  • Selling products or services to community members
  • Multi-level marketing or pyramid schemes
  • Affiliate marketing without disclosure
  • Recruiting for unrelated business opportunities

✅ Permitted:

  • Sharing free resources and tools
  • Recommending apps or services (with disclosure if you benefit)
  • Discussing your profession in relevant contexts

7. PLATFORM-SPECIFIC GUIDELINES

7.1 Discord Server Rules

In addition to these Guidelines, Discord participants must:

  • Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  • Use appropriate channels for different topics
  • Keep conversations in relevant channels
  • Respect channel-specific rules and pinned messages
  • Use Discord's features respectfully (reactions, mentions, etc.)

7.2 Discord Etiquette

  • Use @here and @everyone sparingly and appropriately
  • Keep messages conversational (avoid walls of text)
  • Use thread features for extended discussions
  • Respect others' availability (they may not respond immediately)
